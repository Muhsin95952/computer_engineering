The Holy Quran
The KALAM UL ALLAH


Quran is one of the fourth celestial books of ALLAH. This book is revealed on the last Prophet of ALLAH "hazrat MUHAMMAD (S.A.W).
He is the last Prophet of ALLAH. Quran is also the last book of ALLAH.
It has one hundred and fourteen (114) chapters (surah) and approximately six thousand five hundred and thirty six verses (ayahs).
Some of the chapters of Quran is revealed in MAKKAH (called MAKKI surah) and some are in MADINA (MADANI surah).
Quran is the scriptures of to all human being.
It lays down for them, the law and commandments, code for their social and moral behaviour.
In the Holy Quran ALLAH explain the way of life, rightship of ALLAH, rights of parents, rights of other people.
ALLAH also explain the life and behaviouir of previous nations.
